## Pattern Library — Discovered Observations

### 1. Confounded days show strong synergy, not linear addition
Every confounded day YTD (13 of 35 significant days) produces moves **2-4x larger than simple additive baselines predict**. When forces align, they amplify. Decomposition analysis suggests this is structural, not noise.

**Implication:** Weeks with 2+ aligned catalysts in same direction warrant defensive strike selection — moves will be bigger than per-force baselines suggest.

### 2. Trump Section 232 Tariff arc — classic absorption cycle
- **Jan 14** (announcement): -1.44% (broad exemptions dampened impact)
- **Jan 20** (enforcement): -4.38% (China blocking shipments)
- **Jan 21-22** (digestion): +2.95% / +0.83%
- **Feb onward**: framework priced in, force attenuated within ~7 days

C2 is now ATTENUATING. This is textbook Layer 2 absorption — not because Iran-style economic buffering but because the enforcement mechanism (25% surcharge framework) gave markets a quantifiable price to absorb.

### 3. Iran Conflict arc — multi-month chronic overhang, not acute shock
- **Mar 2** (weekend strikes): gap-down reversal, E2 dominant
- **Mar 6** (oil +35% WoW): risk-off cascade
- **Mar 20-26** (YTD low): chronic risk premium drag
- **Apr 7/8** (ceasefire + AI chip license withdrawal): structural absorption completed

E2 remains ACTIVE but direction flipped. Iran force decayed exactly as the Structural Absorption framework predicted — longer timeline than tariffs because no quantifiable resolution mechanism until the ceasefire.

### 4. F1 Narrative Validation as multiplier, not additive force
Three Tier 4 F1 events (Marvell Mar 31, TSMC Apr 10/17, Intel Apr 24) in last 25 days. F1 multiplier now 1.15x. This is **why the Intel earnings moved NVDA 4.32% today** despite Intel being a competitor — the AI data center validation signal dominates the competitive read on a single-day basis.

### 5. Hyperscaler Capex Cycle is the structural bullish anchor
A1 contributes +12.73 of the +18.32 net bullish YTD. Every hyperscaler earnings cycle and F1 validation event reinforces it. Dominance will compress only if hyperscaler capex guidance itself breaks — no sign of that yet.

### 6. NVDA Earnings is a planned-volatility window, not a tail event
The Q4 FY26 print produced the largest 3-day attributed cluster YTD (-8.21% Feb 25–27). Pre-earnings drift trended modestly bullish on positioning; the print itself delivered a fundamental beat that was overwhelmed by narrative (China DC revenue gap) and technical (50/100-day MA break) factors; post-earnings continuation lasted ~1 trading day before stabilizing.

**Implication for option strike selection:** NVDA earnings is a *planned* volatility event, not a tail risk. Strike+DTE selection should explicitly position relative to the confirmed earnings date — either fully shielded (Earnings Shield, no contract life spans the print) or deliberately bracketed (DTE chosen so the post-earnings IV crush + drift acts as the exit mechanism). When IR confirms a date that displaces the earnings catalyst from an open contract's life, **Mode 4 Calendar Correction** is the authorized response.

**Implication for CSP timing:** Pre-earnings drift windows are the worst phase for new CSP entry — IV is elevated but compensating for binary risk you don't want to take, and channel geometry is distorted by positioning. Post-earnings drift windows are the methodology's natural CSP entry environment (clean regime, channel resolves, Turnaround Tuesday setups can form on follow-through pullbacks).
